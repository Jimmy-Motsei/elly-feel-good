EllyFeelGood: AI‑Powered Assistant for Growth & Compassionate Care

Delivering 24/7 customer support and guidance with empathy and expertise.
Presented by: Maru Online — AI Automation

Executive Summary

  • WhatsApp is a strong start, but replies depend on staff availability and business hours.
  • The AI Assistant works alongside WhatsApp and your website to guide customers, answer questions, book appointments, and suggest the right products — any time.
  • Expect faster replies, more bookings, higher product sales, and better retention through guided care journeys.
  • Built to be safe, empathetic, and ready to hand over to a real person whenever needed.

Why Move Beyond WhatsApp Alone

Current Challenges

  • Customers wait for replies after hours or during peak times.
  • Staff repeat the same answers, causing inconsistency and fatigue.
  • Details are not captured in an organized way for future care.
  • Hard to measure and improve the experience.

How the AI Assistant Helps

  • Instant replies 24/7 with a consistent, caring tone.
  • Collects key details in an organized way for better follow‑ups.
  • Guides customers to book, buy, or request a consultation.
  • Frees staff from repetitive questions.

Understanding the Customers We Serve

  • Chemotherapy‑related hair loss: gentle guidance and quick connection to a human specialist.
  • Alopecia / traction hair loss: clear self‑care education, safe product suggestions, referrals when appropriate.
  • General growth & maintenance: routine guidance, product pairings, education.
  • Loyal buyers: personalized reminders, subscriptions, ongoing encouragement.

Safety, Empathy, and Privacy

  • Customer data handled according to POPIA (South African privacy law): clear opt‑in, minimised data, and deletion on request.
  • No diagnosis or prescriptions; sensitive cases escalate to a human expert.
  • Compassionate, respectful language throughout.
  • Conversation logs enable quality checks and transparency.

How the Experience Works

How the experience flows from entry → consent → intake → guidance → actions → follow-ups.
  • Entry via WhatsApp, website chat, or QR code.
  • Warm welcome, clear purpose, and consent.
  • Friendly questions about goals, concerns, and preferences.
  • Pathways: chemo‑care • other hair loss • general care.
  • Guidance, product suggestions, and easy actions (book or buy).
  • Follow‑ups for progress checks, reminders, and support.

Seamless Human Collaboration

  • Start with the AI Assistant; handover to a human in the same WhatsApp thread when needed.
  • Staff see the full conversation history, so customers never repeat themselves.
  • Appointments sync to your calendar with automatic reminders.

What the Assistant Does for EllyFeelGood

  • Warm greeting and brief, friendly questions.
  • Qualifies and schedules consultations.
  • Suggests product bundles or items based on needs.
  • Provides after‑purchase guidance for correct product use.
  • Sends caring follow‑ups and refill reminders.

How It Fits with Your Business

  • Works with WhatsApp and your website (Instagram later).
  • All interactions connected to your customer records.
  • Supports bookings and payment links to complete actions quickly.
  • Strong privacy and security controls.

Clear and Caring Communication

  • Chemotherapy context: slower, supportive conversation; quick human connection when needed.
  • Alopecia / traction: education on safe practices; guidance for consistent care.
  • Growth & maintenance: daily routines, do’s & don’ts, lifestyle tips.
  • Service recovery: sincere apologies, solutions, and staff escalation.

Measuring Success

  • How quickly customers get their first response.
  • Bookings made compared to today.
  • How often customers accept suggested bundles or return for refills.
  • Customer satisfaction and care sentiment.
  • Hours saved by staff on repetitive questions.

Return on Investment

  • Baseline today: inquiries, bookings, average order value, repeat rate.
  • 24/7 responses → more consultations booked.
  • Relevant suggestions → higher order value.
  • Thoughtful follow‑ups → stronger retention and loyalty.
  • Handling common questions → staff time saved.

  • Data privacy: POPIA compliance and secure handling.
  • Adoption: QR codes, website entry, packaging prompts.
